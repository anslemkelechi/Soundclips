Rider

Juice Wrld

"Rider” is the twenty-first and second to last track from Juice WRLD’s sophomore album Death Race For Love. On the Power-produced track, Juice raps about drugs, as well as wanting to see if his girlfriend, Ally Lotti’s, love is genuine. The track was previewed on Juice WRLD’s Instagram Live on March 5, 2019, three days before the album’s official release.
